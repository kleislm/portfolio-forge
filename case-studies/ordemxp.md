# OrdemXP — "the Duolingo for the bar exam"

🇬🇧 English · [🇧🇷 Português](#-português)

**Role:** Founder · PM · Builder &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Prototype (private) · iOS/Android/Web planned

### What it is
A B2C exam-prep app for Brazil's bar exam (OAB) with **gamification as the product**, not a coat of paint: lives, streaks, a visual learning path, weekly leagues, currency, 1v1 duels, achievements.

### Product decisions (this case is about retention)
- **Gamification *is* the core loop** — loss aversion (streaks/lives) as the engine of habit.
- **Onboarding = level one** (a tutorial disguised as a game) — teach by doing.
- **Mock exams as premium events** with a tactical report (heat map by subject, error analysis by type) — monetization anchored in value.
- **100% B2C** model (individual premium + squads).

### Pillar demonstrated
**Engagement, retention, and B2C monetization** + product-economy design — the opposite of my regulated B2B work, showing range.

## 🇧🇷 Português

**Papel:** Founder · PM · Builder &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Protótipo (privado) · iOS/Android/Web planejados

App B2C de preparação para a OAB com **gamificação como produto**, não como verniz: vidas, ofensivas, trilha visual de aprendizado, ligas semanais, moeda, duelos 1v1, conquistas.

**Engajamento, retenção e monetização B2C** + design de economia de produto — o oposto do meu trabalho B2B regulado, mostrando range.
