### Goal
Generate qualified leads and booked calls for a performance agency selling to the **US market** via paid traffic.

### Growth decisions (the heart of the case)
- **Message match by traffic source** — the page adapts to where the ad click came from (high-intent search vs. creative-led social).
- **Two-step form** to lift completion through micro-commitment.
- **Full UTM capture** stored with each lead — to close the attribution loop.
- **Instrumented events** across the funnel (form start, lead, call click, scroll depth).
- **Production-ready multilingual** with localized URLs — not placeholders.

### Why it matters
Concrete evidence of my growth/performance pillar: not "I know marketing," but **I built the entire conversion asset** — message, friction, instrumentation, attribution — thinking in CAC and lead quality.
