### What it is
A **premium institutional landing page** for a consultancy that structures the operational, financial, legal, and compliance layers of medical clinics.

### Decisions (this case is about craft & positioning)
- **Authority-by-calm positioning:** "clinics don't grow on improvisation — they grow on structure."
- **Editorial, institutional aesthetic** with deliberate geometric visuals instead of stock medical clichés — category differentiation on purpose.
- Refined scarcity and low-friction CTAs, no aggressive banners.

### Pillar demonstrated
**Positioning + copy + art direction** — the part of product that sets perceived value before any feature. Shows range beyond dashboards: I can dress a product.
