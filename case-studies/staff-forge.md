# Staff Forge — hands-on environment to reach Staff/Principal PM

**Role:** Concept · PM · Builder &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Status:** Live

### Thesis
No course turns someone into a Staff PM. You get there by **producing real artifacts**. So I built an environment where progression is by **unlocked capabilities**, not by days of content.

### What's inside (each tool produces a saveable artifact)
- **12-competency self-diagnosis** (Ravi Mehta) → radar chart highlighting the strategy/influence gaps to focus on.
- **RICE/ICE priorization** with a built-in anti-bias warning.
- **Opportunity Solution Tree** (Teresa Torres) — Outcome → Opportunity → Solution → Experiment.
- **Discovery interview builder** focused on past behaviour.
- **Canvas hub:** Lean, JTBD, North Star, simplified Wardley Map.
- **AI eval-suite builder** — test cases + LLM-as-judge config + AI metrics (grounding, hallucination, latency, cost).
- **PR-FAQ** (Working Backwards) + **Strategy Kernel** (Rumelt).
- **Interview case dojo** across product sense, metrics, and strategy.

### Why it matters
It's living proof of my product foundations: I took the state of the art (Cagan, Mehta, Torres, Rumelt, AI-eval practice, Wardley) and turned it into a working product. Meta-competence: I know what defines a senior PM *and* I can operationalise it.
